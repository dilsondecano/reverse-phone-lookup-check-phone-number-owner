What Is a People Finder?

A people finder is an online tool that lets you quickly gather information about someone. Instead of relying on guesswork or scattered search results, you can look up someone online through databases that provide phone numbers, addresses, emails, and background details.

Most people search platforms are designed to help you search for someone in the US, whether you're trying to reconnect with an old friend, verify a new contact, or confirm details before doing business. These tools often make it possible to check public records online, including property ownership, court filings, and previous addresses, which adds an extra layer of reliability.

If you need to know how to find someone's contact details, a people finder can show you current phone numbers, emails, or mailing addresses. Searchbug allows you to find people by name and address, making it easier to update your records or reach the right person without wasting time on outdated information.

How to Use the People Finder Tool

Searchbug's People Search

You don't always need a full name to begin. Reverse people searches can be done with other details, making it easier to look up someone online even if your information is limited.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Enter What You Know

Start with all the details you have—name, address, or phone number.

If you don't get results, try fewer details. For example:

  • Full name + address
  • Full name + city (avoid common names)
  • Address only (remove apartment/unit number if no match)
  • Phone number only

Step 2: Review the Results

Once you run the search, check the results to confirm the person's identity or update your contact list.

You Can Search Using:

  • Phone Number
  • Street Address + City + State
  • Email Address
  • Social Security Number (Add-On Required)
  • Driver's License Number (Add-On Required)

Best Search Combinations

  • First + Last Name + Date of Birth
  • Last Name + Street + City + State
  • First + Last Name + State
  • Last Name + City + State

Tip: When searching by name, being specific avoids broad or inaccurate results. The street address should include a house number—searches by street name only won't display all residents. You can also use an old phone number or past address to find updated or more recent contact details, like a newer address or other associated phone numbers.
